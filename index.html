About Me

My full names are Cyrus Nonde. I was born in Lusaka, at the University Teaching Hospitals. I spent most of my young life in Chilanga, Zambia, but was originally raised in Lilanda, Matero. Growing up in Matero was difficult; noisy bars, noisy passers-by; something had to change. We moved to Chilanga in 2012, during the original mitigations of the Digital Divide, computers were slowly distributed to all competent users with monetary funds to purchase them. This gave me a chance to develop Computer-literacy. Web development took me by the storm during high-school, and I thought learning HTML and CSS would teach me how to hack devices, webpages, and servers. I learnt many selectors, tags, elements and classes, their :pseudo conterparts and how to style them. As a Web Designer and Security Technician, my goal is to increase Computer-literacy and design the best Webpages America has to see. I have a Regera.
